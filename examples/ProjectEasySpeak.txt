EasySpeak is an improved version of AAC software. It features a personalized agent based on information about the user that it has gathered from the user’s previous conversations. This product will have a UI that is customizable based on the user’s abilities. The team working on it are all Computer Science majors at BYU that are doing sandbox, a school sponsored startup incubator. Danny is the face of the project and has been helping with the design process along the way by helping us brainstorm and giving us feedback. The team is working with advisors from other AI startups. Their mission is to build a tool that allows their customers to communicate more effectively than was ever possible before.
